  Dear Friend, 

The Slavic Art Gallery has been created to help Slavic artists to show you interesting, emotional and professional paintings, drawings and design.

Since the economic condition in their countries makes it very hard to send their works, they have continued to work and wait for a good chance to show you their creations.

They would like to show you their soul in pictures and to share their energy with you.  Artists are positive thinking people who believe that the Arts will save the Peace in the World.

We want you to have a wonderful time enjoying life, looking through the eyes of the artists.
