VLADIMIR RADIN
Saint Petersburg, Russia

Slavic Art Gallery

 Vladimir Radin - is a professional artist, who has been working more than twenty years in painting, drawing, and design. He graduated from the Mukhinskoye Institute of Fine Arts, St. Petersburg, Russia in 1984. 
Vladimir is famous for his special manner and unique style. Many professionals consider him a Great Master of textural technique. 

The artist has participated in the following International Exhibitions: 
France: Paris - 1991, 1997 
Germany: Strasbourg - 1991, Erfurd - 1993 
Norway: Gavle, Umea, Lulea, Kiruna, Goteborg - 1996 
Russia: Moscow - 1994, 1998; St. Petersburg - every year from 1984
